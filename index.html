"what made you happy today?" explore responses to this question from thousands of humans, across demographics. this data was sourced from HappyDB, a crowd-sourced database of 100,000 happy moments. each moment was sorted into one of seven categories, the distribution of which is reflected in the relative sizes of the categories below.

ABOUT

HappyDB is a database built by researchers at Meganon Labs. The text contents of each happy moment, along with all corresponding demographic information, was collected via Amazon's Mechanical Turk, a crowdsourcing marketplace. The relevant dataset and paper can be found on Meganon Lab's website here.
